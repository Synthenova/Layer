The Philosophy

Chemistry
& Artistry

Traditional perfumes are static. Your mood is not. Scent stacking allows you to modulate intensity and complexity. Start with a hydrating base to lock in molecules, layer a woody core, and finish with a citrus top note.

The Notes

Top Notes 15-30 Mins

The first impression. Typically citrus, light florals, or herbs. They evaporate quickly but set the mood.

Heart Notes 2-4 Hours

The core of the fragrance. Spices, deeper florals, and fruits emerge once the top notes fade.

Base Notes 6+ Hours

The foundation. Woods, musk, amber, and vanilla. These rich molecules linger the longest on your skin.
